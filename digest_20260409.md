## ASEAN Cash Management Intelligence — Week of 09 April 2026

### 🏦 Competitor Moves

• **DBS** has launched API-enabled FAST real-time SGD payments up to SGD 200,000 via its IDEAL RAPID platform, enabling instant 24x7 corporate settlement across participating Singapore banks. DBS is also promoting a Bill Payments solution with multi-channel collections (iBanking, digibank, PayLah!, AXS) and automated ERP integration for corporates with high consumer payment volumes.

• **HSBC** won Euromoney's 2025 best cash management bank awards for Asia and Singapore, leveraging its 600+ APAC branches and positioning Singapore as a gateway for ASEAN expansion with integrated payments, pooling, and liquidity solutions. The bank is targeting internationally-minded corporates across the region's 4,200+ Regional Treasury Centres concentrated in Singapore.

• **Deutsche Bank** launched dbX, a next-generation modular treasury platform integrating accounts, payments, transactional FX, and liquidity management in real time for corporates, NBFIs, and fintechs globally. **BCA Indonesia** continues to set the competitive benchmark domestically with a comprehensive Cash Management suite accessible via business e-banking and API channels, leveraging 1,270 branches and 43 million customer accounts.
