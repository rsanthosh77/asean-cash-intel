### ⚡ So What for Transaction Banks

- **API-enabled real-time payments**: Transaction banks must accelerate integration of domestic instant payment rails (FAST, PromptPay, InstaPay) into corporate API banking channels to match DBS's SGD200,000 instant payment capability and deliver 24x7 payment certainty that drives corporate client retention in Singapore, Thailand, and Philippines markets.

- **One-leg-out cross-border strategy**: Prioritize OLO models leveraging existing ASEAN instant payment linkages (PayNow-PromptPay) over building new infrastructure to deliver faster, cheaper cross-border payments that meet G20 2027 targets and compete with global banks' instant payment strategies across Singapore-Thailand-Malaysia corridors.

- **Treasury platform modernization**: Invest in unified, modular treasury platforms integrating real-time accounts, payments, FX, and liquidity management with AI and digital asset readiness to compete with Deutsche Bank's dbX and Standard Chartered's multi-rail offerings for multinational and regional corporate treasury mandates.
