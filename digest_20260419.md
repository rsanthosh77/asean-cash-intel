## ASEAN Cash Management Intelligence — Week of 19 April 2026

### 🏦 Competitor Moves

**Standard Chartered** is building ASEAN transaction banking around real-time domestic rails (PayNow, PromptPay, DuitNow, QRIS), Project Nexus cross-border linkage, tokenised deposits for 24/7 settlement, and Partior partnership for real-time multi-currency clearing. (Source: The Asian Banker, 9 Apr, https://www.theasianbanker.com/updates-and-articles/standard-chartered-builds-transaction-banking-around-asean-s-cross-border-complexity)

**Standard Chartered** launched SC PrismFX, a transactional FX solution offering seamless global FX payments with connectivity to local payment schemes and wallets for corporates, financial institutions, and non-bank financial institutions. (Source: Standard Chartered, 17 Apr 2025, https://www.sc.com/en/corporate-investment-banking/transaction-banking/scprismfx)

**DBS** offers FAST real-time SGD payments (up to SGD200,000 per transaction) accessible via IDEAL RAPID API, enabling corporate customers to make instant 24x7 payments with immediate fund availability and transaction status within minutes. (Source: DBS, date unverified — treat with caution, https://www.dbs.com.sg/documents/1038650/59715708/Low+Value+Real-Time+Payments+%28FAST%29.pdf/0e853f12-037d-ea46-5940-6f6f605e230c?t=1601267753692)

### 💡 Innovation Signals

Mastercard has launched authenticated agentic payment pilots in Singapore and Malaysia, with plans to expand across ASEAN and establish a regional AI Centre of Excellence in Singapore. (Source: Mastercard, 7 Apr, https://fintechnews.sg/128562/ai/mastercard-agentic-payments-asean/)

(Source: PT Bank Central Asia Tbk, date unverified — treat with caution, https://www.bca.co.id/-/media/Files/2023/20230927-final-rev-riplay-umum-api-bca-2109023-pdf-des)

### ⚡ So What for Transaction Banks

**Real-Time Cross-Border Payments:** ASEAN cross-border QR payments reached 36.2 million transactions worth US$716.4 million in 2025 across 29 linkages, creating competitive threat to correspondent banking channels but opportunity for transaction banks to provide liquidity, FX, and reconciliation services for corporate treasuries managing multi-country collections. (Source: ASEAN Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors, 13 Apr, https://fintechnews.sg/129690/payments/asean-qr-payments/)

**API-Enabled Corporate Liquidity Management:** Standard Chartered's proven API deployment reducing payment clearing from four days to near real-time signals that ASEAN corporate clients and development finance institutions will increasingly demand API-first, real-time payment automation and reconciliation beyond legacy SWIFT and batch clearing channels. (Source: Standard Chartered, 8 Sep 2025, https://www.sc.com/en/uploads/sites/66/content/docs/Standard-Chartered-Case-Study-Uganda-Development-Bank.pdf)

**Indonesia Collections Infrastructure:** BCA's SNAP-compliant API platform and integrated Business Collection suite (QRIS, Payment Link, e-Commerce acquiring) set competitive baseline for embedded banking and merchant acquiring that transaction banks must match to compete for corporate cash management mandates in Indonesia's digital payments market. (Source: PT Bank Central Asia Tbk, date unverified — treat with caution, https://www.bca.co.id/-/media/Files/2023/20230927-final-rev-riplay-umum-api-bca-2109023-pdf-des)
